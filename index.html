Purnia is a city that serves as the administrative headquarters of both Purnia district and Purnia division in the Indian state of Bihar.


Bhawanipur Block Population, Caste, Religion Data - Purnia district, Bihar



Bhawanipur Block of Purnia district has total population of 161,720 as per the Census 2011. Out of which 84,098 are males while 77,622 are females. In 2011 there were total 30,772 families residing in Bhawanipur Block. The Average Gender Ratio of Bhawanipur Block is 923.
